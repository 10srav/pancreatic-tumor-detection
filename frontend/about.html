About The Project

Pancreatic cancer is one of the most aggressive forms of cancer, often detected at late stages. This project aims to assist radiologists by providing an automated, AI-powered second opinion for detecting pancreatic tumors from CT scans, potentially leading to earlier diagnosis and better patient outcomes.
